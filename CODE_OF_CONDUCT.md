# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus on technical outcomes and user impact.
- Accept feedback and collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, baiting, or sustained disruption.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Intimidating, abusive, or threatening conduct.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting
Report incidents privately to project maintainers through available repository contact channels.

## Scope
This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and any project-managed communication spaces.
